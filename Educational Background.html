My Educational Background

Junior High School

I went to Valenzuela National Highschool for my Junior Highschool. I was in a special program for 4 years. The special program is like STEM however there it was called STEP.
STEP was an acronym for Science, Technology, and Engineering Program. It was a special course that required an entrance examination in order to get in. In that time the slot was very limited.
During my grade 7 to 8, those were the years that I had a hard time keeping up due to the tasks that they keep giving out.
Before the end of my grade 8 year, that was when the Pandemic hit so my grade 9th and 10th years were all online. With 2 months left
My batch were lucky to have a face to face graduation. I finished grade 7 to grade 10 in VALNAT.

Senior High School

I went to CIIT Senior High School for my senior High School. I finished my grade 11 and grade 12 year here. I took a programming strand and continued to pursue this path.
When I went here, I learned various things like preparing myself into the real world. With subjects industry readiness I was prepared to go to my OJT.
I learned Game Development, Database, Web Design, basic Programming, and other more coding related subjects that helped me with my OJT.

College

I am currently enrolled at CIIT College of Arts and Technology as a Bachelor of Computer Science freshman.
